GLOBAL SHARK ATTACK DASHBOARD 2000-2018

This dashboard is interactive. If you want to see a particular value, you can hover over a bar or slice on a pie chart, and the value will appear. Alternatively, if you would like to see the relevant information linked to a certain country for example, click on the country in the bar chart, and all the other charts will show the information for that country. If you are unable to see the full chart, please scroll to the right. There are also a selection of filters available at the top of the page you can use to filter the charts. To reset all charts, go to the bottom of the page and click on the reset button.
