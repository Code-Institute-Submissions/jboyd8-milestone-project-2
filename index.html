Global Shark Attack Dashboard 2000-2018

Insturctions

This dashboard is interactive. If you want to see a particular value, you can hover over a bar or slice on a pie chart, and the value will appear. Alternatively, if you would like to see the relevant information linked to a certain country for example, click on the country in the bar chart, and all the other charts will show the information for that country. To reset all charts, go to the bottom of the page and click on the reset button.
